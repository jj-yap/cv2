Undergraduate Thesis

Design and Simulation of a Horizontal-Axis Tidal Turbine (HATT) Blade for Tropical Site Conditions

The goal of the study was to create horizontal axis tidal turbines suited for use in waters with low velocity conditions like those found in the Philippines. The study used Telemac2D to simulate the tidal conditions experienced in Davao Gulf. A HATT blade profiled was then generated using HARP_opt from National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL). The generated blade profile was then modeled using Solidworks, after which it is subjected to a computational fluid dynamic simulation to measure its performance. The paper was written alongside Kenneth R. Morales and Engr. Mark Anthony Rotor.
